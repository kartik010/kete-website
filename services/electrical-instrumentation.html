Electrical & instrumentation

Power distribution, motor control, and field instrumentation engineered for safety, repeatability, and compliance — from refinery-grade projects to precision commercial builds. We speak the same language as your maintenance team and your consultant.

Scope we cover

Design support, execution, or both — integrated with your solar or standalone electrical programmes. Documentation is structured for audits, insurer review, and future expansion without reverse-engineering the panel.

  • HT/LT panels, MCCs, distribution boards, and bus routing
  • Cable sizing, tray work, grounding, and harmonics-aware layouts
  • Field instruments: flow, pressure, temperature, level; loop checks and calibration discipline
  • Control integration signals to PLC/DCS boundaries where the project defines them

Quality and compliance

We align with applicable Indian standards, client engineering specs, and site safety programmes. FAT/SAT support, test sheets, and as-built red-lines are part of the deliverable — not an afterthought before handover.

Where we add the most value

Brownfield upgrades, parallel new construction, and sites where solar DC and AC must coexist cleanly with existing protection. If your bottleneck is coordination between civil, electrical, and OEM vendors, we step in as the thread that ties schedules together.

Send SLDs, load lists, or a one-line problem statement — we will propose a clear electrical and instrumentation path.
