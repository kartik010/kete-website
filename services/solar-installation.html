Solar installation

End-to-end solar EPC for organisations that need predictable generation, clean documentation, and systems that still perform years after handover — from megawatt-scale rooftops to community and industrial plants across India and Nepal.

What we deliver

We engineer and build photovoltaic systems as a single accountable partner: load and shade studies, plant layout, structural and electrical integration, procurement, installation, testing, and commissioning aligned with local grid and safety norms.

  • Commercial & industrial rooftop and elevated structures
  • Ground-mount and distributed generation where site conditions allow
  • Grid-tie integration, protection coordination, and SCADA-ready monitoring hooks
  • As-built documentation, O&M handover, and warranty-aligned snag closure

Who it is for

Manufacturing, logistics, refineries, institutions, and developers who want a partner that has already delivered in harsh electrical environments — not a catalogue reseller. We align CAPEX, generation targets, and compliance early so change orders do not appear at energisation.

How we work

  • Discover — Feasibility, roof or land survey, sanctioning and DISCOM dialogue as needed.
  • Design — Electrical single-line, stringing, inverter selection, and loss budgets you can audit.
  • Build — Civil, DC cabling, AC works, and testing with documented checkpoints.
  • Hand over — Training, spares list, and performance baseline for your O&M team or ours.

Share load data, location, and target COD — we will respond with a realistic path to energisation.
